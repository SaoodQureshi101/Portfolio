Small Business Booking Flow

Role: Product designer

Timeline: 4 weeks

Goal: Increase completed bookings without requiring more support calls.

Problem

Users abandoned the booking process when pricing details appeared late and slot availability felt uncertain. Mobile users faced especially high drop-off due to long forms and hidden progress state.

Process

  • Analyzed funnel analytics and session replays to identify friction points
  • Reordered flow: service, time, confirmation, payment
  • Added pricing summary and cancellation policy in-context
  • Prototyped reduced form fields and tested with 10 target users

Outcome

Completed bookings increased by 27% over 3 weeks. Mobile completion improved most, rising from 31% to 52%. Support contacts related to booking uncertainty dropped by 18%.

What I would improve next

Add calendar sync and reminder preferences to reduce no-shows and improve post-booking confidence.
